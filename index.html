How to Install the Forerunner 255 Update

Step-by-Step Update Guide

  1. Charge Your Watch: Ensure your Forerunner 255 has at least 50% battery.
  2. Connect to Garmin Connect: Open the app on your paired smartphone.
  3. Check for Updates: Navigate to More > Settings > Device > System Update.
  4. Download the Update: Select "Install Update" if available.
  5. Transfer to Your Watch: Keep devices close during transfer (15-25 minutes).
  6. Complete Installation: Watch will install automatically - don't interrupt.
  7. Restart Your Watch: Device may restart to finalize changes.

Wi-Fi Update Option: Enable Wi-Fi in settings for automatic updates when available.
